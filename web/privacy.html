ANCHORAGE

Privacy Policy
Effective: 25 February 2026

ANCHORAGE ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Urge logs — timestamps and intensity ratings of recorded urges
  • Relapse logs — timestamps and optional notes for relapse entries
  • Reflection entries — free-text journal entries you write during reflections
  • Personal values — your selected core values (up to 3)
  • First name — used only for personalising the app experience locally
  • App preferences — your guarded app selections and settings

This data remains on your device and is deleted when you clear the app's data or uninstall ANCHORAGE.

1.2 Data Synced via Firebase

We use Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in) and Cloud Firestore to sync minimal data for streak tracking and accountability features:

  • Anonymous user ID (UUID) — a randomly generated identifier with no connection to your real identity
  • Streak data — your current streak count and start date
  • Accountability partner details — if you choose to add an accountability partner, their name and email address are stored in Firestore to enable weekly progress reports

We do not collect your real name, phone number, physical address, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) through Firebase.

1.3 Data Processed by Third-Party Services

  • SendGrid — if you add an accountability partner, we use SendGrid to send weekly progress reports to your partner's email address. SendGrid processes the recipient's email address and the report content (streak data and general progress, never urge/relapse details). See SendGrid's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — if you subscribe to ANCHORAGE+, RevenueCat processes your subscription through the Google Play Store. We receive your subscription status but never your payment card details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above solely to:

  • Provide and personalise the ANCHORAGE app experience
  • Track and display your streak progress
  • Send accountability reports to your chosen partner
  • Process subscription payments through RevenueCat
  • Improve app stability and fix bugs (anonymous crash data only)

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history or the domains you visit
  • We do not collect the content of blocked pages
  • We do not use analytics trackers, advertising SDKs, or fingerprinting
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing

4. VPN and Content Filtering

ANCHORAGE uses a local VPN service to filter DNS requests on your device. All DNS filtering happens entirely on your device. No browsing data, DNS queries, or network traffic is transmitted to our servers. The VPN routes only DNS queries through a local tunnel — your actual internet traffic is not intercepted or proxied.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data — retained on your device until you clear app data, uninstall, or sign out
  • Firebase data — retained while your anonymous account exists. If you request deletion, we will remove all Firestore data associated with your anonymous ID within 30 days
  • SendGrid — email delivery logs are retained by SendGrid per their retention policy (typically 30 days)
  • RevenueCat — subscription records are retained per RevenueCat's data retention policy and Google Play requirements

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Australian Privacy Act)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that we delete all data associated with your account
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent — stop data processing at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Security Rules restricting data access to authenticated users
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communication
  • Minimal data collection principle — we only collect what is strictly necessary
  • No server-side storage of sensitive personal data (urge logs, reflections, relapse logs)

8. Children's Privacy

ANCHORAGE is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through an in-app notice. The "Effective" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.
